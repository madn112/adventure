Lineage of Kings

The Lineage of Kings after the Great Rebellion of Aderisa goes as follows:

King Cain Aedelwald I Reigned 243-293 before being assassinated by rogue agents from the Republic of Aderitrem

King Elyas Aedelwald I Reigned 293-300 before the throne was usurped by his younger brother

King Alper Snowshod I Reigned 300-301 only reigned one year after the usurpation as he was a lousy and lazy king

King Admon Aedelwald I Reigned 301-350 reigned 49 years before peacefully in his sleep

King Cain Aedelwald II Reigned 351-421 reigned 70 years before dying from poison in his drink

King Mathias Snowshod I Reigned 421-500 died peacfully in his sleep

After King Mathias Snowshod had died the Kingdom Split into three in the Center expanding out westward was the Kingdom of Aderisa ruled by King Alper Snowshod II, spreading out from the stretches of the North was the Kingdom of Eleralla ruled by King Cain Aedelwald III, and the Kingdom to the South named Glardolath was ruled by Queen Theresa Marnalis

After a Great War between the three siblings Queen Theresa Marnalis had won and cut off both siblings Lineages thus ending the Family Aedelwald and Snowshod Queen Theresa Marnalis was succeeded by her son and heir King Rolfe Marnalis I

Our Current Queen is the Daughter of King Rolfe and is Queen Calista Marnalis I and has ruled from 0-present

Castles

There are various castles amongst the Kingdom of Aderisa the famous of which is Fortress Cainfield when in 243 B.G. (Before Gunpowder) the battle of Cainfield took place between the forces of Cain and his soldiers of Aderisa and Abel and his soldiers of Aderitrem. Abel who was the military commander of the Aderitrem was trying to put down the rebellion started by his brother Cain. During the Battle an arrow loosed by Cain struck Abel in the heart and he died there instantly. Upon seeing their commander fall in battle the forces of the Aderitrem routed and fell back all the way to the capital of Aldwald.
