Path Away from Village

You and your Companions Onthalla and Markus have been travelling for 2 days to reach the City of Cainlide before the King of Mandeiras can behead you all for treason.

After you hear the rustling of the leaves you ask who's there you get no response but hear the rasp of a blade sliding from the scabbard

Before you can respond with your own steel your attacker stabs you 2 times in the leg you reach out only to see Markus standing there with his blade with your blood on it.

You Are Dead
