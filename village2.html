The Empire of Mandeiras

The Empire of Mandeiras is on western edge of the Sea of Storms it's capital Dragoncrag Rock is near the Bay of Eels established near the year 0 B.G. The Kingdom of Mandeiras is one of the oldest Kingdoms in the world. Despite this it's Army is still reeling from it's major defeat at the Battle of Baronbone Briar against the Elvish armies of Adwendagan It's Merchant fleets the largest in the world but constantly onslaughted by the Massive warships of Adwendagan causing them to lose more ships to pirates and privateers. On land as well the Kingdom deals with rogues and rebels who surround their capital. These are why the other Kingdoms call Mandeiras the Old Man Kingdom due to it's slow rather than sudden collapse.

Lineage of Emperors

The Lineage of Emperors after the Great Founding of Mandeiras goes as follows:

Emperor Mandeiras Marticio I Reigned 12th of Morndal 0 BG to 15th of Morndal 80 BG before being assassinated by religious zealots of the Cult of Kosmosimi

Emperor Manderio Marticio I Reigned from 81 BG after retaking the Capital from the Cult of Kosmosimi to 140 BG before dying peacefully in his sleep he willed the Kingdom to his younger and to his older he willed the wealth he had attained to go create his own Kingdom.

Empress Mintalio Marticio I Reigned from 140 BG to 200 BG she never got married but had one female Bastard who she admitted was hers and gave her heirship.

Empress Mintalio Marticio II Reigned from 200 BG to 248 BG she was killed by a disgruntled servant who had killed off the main lineage of Marticio Emperors and after that dark day the Cadet branches of the Marticios took over and soon civil war was upon the Empire.

Emperor Silvestro D'Agostino Reigned 248 BG to 300 BG he was killed in the streets of Dragoncrag Rock by an angry mob of Aderisian refugee's.

Emperor Carlo D'Agostino Reigned 300 BG to 1 AG he thought he was safe on the tower walls away from the battle for his kingdom but a stray bullet lodged in his throat killing him near instantly

After Emperor Carlo D'Agostino something now known as the War of the Asters. Asters were the royal symbol of the D'Agostinos and the Asters and the Agostos. This was a three way Civil War in the empire between the Cadet branches of the former Marticio Emperors. The war was bloody and loud as gunpowder had been discovered by Imperial scientists about 1 year earlier and although other Kingdoms and Republics didn't know about the invention yet they would as the war eventually spanned the entire world engulfing it all in Fire and Sword. Eventually the Emperor that came out on top was the Asters and their Elven allies in the winning Siege of Dragoncrag Rock.

So far only the Asters remain as the other branches either died in battle from disease or from assassination or suicide. The Asters are now no longer a cadet branch and are now the Main Imperial leaders but under a Matron lineage where only females could rule.

Our current Empress, Empress Vaermina Asters has been Regining from 6 AG to present and while her reign has been relatively peaceful war looms over the horizon as I fear our world maybe larger than once thought.
